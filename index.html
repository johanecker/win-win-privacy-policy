Privacy Policy for Win Win Extension

Effective Date: June 12, 2024

Introduction

Thank you for using Win Win. This privacy policy explains how our Chrome extension collects, uses, and shares user data. By using the Win Win extension, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Data Collection and Usage

1. Information We Collect:

Tabs and Windows Data: Win Win requires access to your browser's tabs and windows to provide the core functionality of splitting and merging windows. This includes information about open tabs, active tab status, and window dimensions.

2. How We Use Your Information:

Functionality: The collected data is used solely to perform the extension's main functions, such as splitting your current browser window into two side-by-side windows and merging multiple windows into one.

User Experience: Information about your tabs and windows helps us provide a seamless user experience by ensuring accurate and efficient window management.

Data Sharing

1. Third-Party Sharing:

No Sharing: Win Win does not share, sell, or distribute any user data to third parties. All data collected is used solely for the functionality of the extension and remains on your device.

Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Win Win implements reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this privacy policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
